## 1. Welcome to the AgriManagerX Partner Program

AgriManagerX is an offline-first farm management platform built for smallholder farmers across Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, and Latin America. We help farmers track livestock, manage finances, record harvests, run payroll, and monitor inventory — all from a smartphone, even without internet.

As a **Commission Sales Partner**, you earn recurring commissions by introducing AgriManagerX to farms, agribusinesses, cooperatives, and agricultural input dealers in your territory. You are an independent contractor — not an employee — and you set your own schedule.

## 6. How to Sell AgriManagerX

### Step 1 — Identify Prospects
Target farms that already keep some form of records (notebook, Excel) or that deal with:
- 500+ layers or 1,000+ broilers
- Dairy cattle or mixed livestock
- Fish ponds of any size
- Crop farms with hired labor (payroll is a strong hook)
- Cooperatives managing multiple member farms

### Step 2 — Open the Conversation
Use the WhatsApp pitch or say this in person:

> *"I came across an app that helps farmers track their flocks and finances on their phone — even without internet. It calculates payroll automatically and shows your profit per flock. A lot of farmers are saving hours every week. Can I show you a 5-minute demo?"*

### Step 3 — Run the Demo
Show these three things — they close most deals:
1. **Daily entry** — how fast it is to record eggs/mortality/feed (under 60 seconds)
2. **Dashboard** — profit/loss and production trends at a glance
3. **Payroll** — automatically calculate worker salaries from attendance

### Step 4 — Offer the Trial
Give the farmer your 30-day Pro trial link. No credit card required.

### Step 5 — Follow Up and Convert
Follow up after 7 days. Ask: *"Have you been using it? What has been most useful?"* Help them set up one enterprise properly. Farmers who complete setup almost always convert to Pro.

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to be a tech expert?**
No. If you can use WhatsApp, you can demonstrate AgriManagerX. We will walk you through the demo in your onboarding call.

**Q: What if a farmer I referred cancels and re-subscribes later?**
If they re-subscribe within 6 months, you still earn the commission. After 6 months, the referral is released and may be claimed by another partner.

**Q: Can I recruit sub-partners under me?**
Not at this time. All partners operate at the same tier. We may introduce a team model in the future for Gold Partners.

**Q: What if a farmer contacts AgriManagerX directly after I introduced them?**
As long as they signed up using your referral link at any point, or you registered them in your partner dashboard before they subscribed, the commission is yours.

**Q: Is the commission taxable?**
In most countries, yes. You are responsible for declaring commission income per your local tax laws. We will provide a monthly earnings statement you can use for your records.

**Q: How do I know my referrals are being tracked correctly?**
Your partner dashboard shows every click, trial signup, and conversion linked to your referral link. If you believe a sale was missed, contact us within 30 days with the farmer's email or phone number.
